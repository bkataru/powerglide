"**Powerglide**" is a single by American [hip hop](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hip_hop_music "Hip hop music") duo [Rae Sremmurd](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rae_Sremmurd "Rae Sremmurd"). It was released on March 1, 2018 by [EarDrummers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/EarDrummers_Entertainment "EarDrummers Entertainment") and [Interscope Records](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interscope_Records "Interscope Records") as the third single from their third studio album *[SR3MM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SR3MM "SR3MM")* (2018).[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-1)[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-2) The song is written by the artists alongside producers Mally Mall, Jean-Marie Horvat, and [Mike Will Made It](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Will_Made_It "Mike Will Made It").[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-Tidal-3) The song features guest vocals from American rapper [Juicy J](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juicy_J "Juicy J") (although he is notably cut from the radio edit).

## Background

\[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Powerglide_\(song\)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Background")\]

The song was released alongside two other singles from *SR3MM*, [Swae Lee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swae_Lee "Swae Lee")'s "Hurt to Look" and Slim Jxmmi's "Brxnks Truck", on March 1, 2018.[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-4)[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-5) The album is a triple-disc album featuring *SR3MM* and solo albums from Rae Sremmurd members [Swae Lee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swae_Lee "Swae Lee"), titled *Swaecation*, and Slim Jxmmi, titled *Jxmtro*.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-6) The song samples [Three 6 Mafia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Three_6_Mafia "Three 6 Mafia")'s "[Side 2 Side](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Side_2_Side "Side 2 Side")", featuring Mafia member Juicy J.[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-7) They were able to get Juicy J involved after a run-in at a party. Juicy expressed an interest in collaboration and went to the studio. He sent his part back, and as Swae says, "wrecked it."[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-8)

## Music video

\[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Powerglide_\(song\)&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Music video")\]

A music video for "Powerglide" was released on March 12, 2018.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-9) In the visual, the rappers perform the track up on a parking structure rooftop as cars burn rubber, do donuts and get "lit up" by overhead helicopters. Producer Mike Will Made It makes a cameo appearance in the video.[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Powerglide_\(song\)#cite_note-10)
