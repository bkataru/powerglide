About

Song Bio4 contributors

“Powerglide” appears on the Rae Sremmurd side of the duo’s highly anticipated [*SR3MM*](https://genius.com/albums/Rae-sremmurd/Sremmlife-3-sr3mm) triple-disc album.

Leading up to its release, this song was heavily teased on social media. In early February, a music video was shot on a rooftop in Los Angeles.

The song samples [Three 6 Mafia](https://genius.com/artists/Three-6-mafia)’s [“Side 2 Side”](https://genius.com/Three-6-mafia-side-2-side-lyrics) and features Mafia member Juicy J.

It was released alongside 2 other singles from *SremmLife 3* – Swae Lee’s [“Hurt to Look”](https://genius.com/Swae-lee-hurt-to-look-lyrics) and Slim Jxmmi’s [“Brxnks Truck”](https://genius.com/Slim-jxmmi-brxnks-truck-lyrics) – online across all streaming platforms on March 1, 2018, giving fans 1 single from each of the [project’s 3 sides](http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/01/rae-sremmurd-sremmlife-3-triple-album/).

Is this the first time any of these artists have collaborated?

Genius Answer

No, the trio has collaborated multiple times before. Juicy J featured Rae Sremmurd on his song [“Already”](https://genius.com/Juicy-j-already-lyrics) in 2015 and Rae Sremmurd featured Juicy J on their song [“Shake It Fast”](https://genius.com/Rae-sremmurd-shake-it-fast-lyrics) in 2016.

Prototype cars, not a Jaguar…
“Powerglide” samples Three 6 Mafia’s “Side 2 Side” and features Mafia member Juicy J. The track is part of, SR3MM, Rae Sremmurd’s triple-disc album.

In the song, Swae Lee refers to a Lamborghini with a Powerglide transmission (a two-speed transmission meant for drag racing.) “Slime green” and “peanut butter” refer to the car’s exterior/interior colors.
